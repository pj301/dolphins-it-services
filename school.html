Main System Features

Setup Module
Academic Year

Define and manage academic calendars, including start and end dates, semesters, and breaks, ensuring alignment with educational timelines.

Department

Organize institutional structure by creating and managing departments (e.g., Science, Arts), facilitating role-based access and resource allocation.

Program

Configure degree or course programs (e.g., Bachelor of Science, Early Childhood Education), linking them to curricula and student enrollment.

Sections

Manage class divisions within programs, enabling assignment of students and instructors for efficient classroom management.

Rooms

Track and assign physical or virtual classrooms, including capacity and availability, to optimize scheduling.

Discount

Set up various discount schemes (e.g., sibling discounts, merit-based) to support student affordability and retention.

Miscellaneous

Handle additional fees or charges (e.g., library fines, extracurricular costs) for flexible financial management.

Laboratory

Manage lab resources, schedules, and usage for science and technical courses, ensuring safety and availability.

Early Enrollment

Facilitate pre-registration processes, allowing students to secure spots and plan ahead for the upcoming term.

Other Fees

Capture additional charges like uniform costs or field trip fees, enhancing revenue tracking.

Other Income

Record non-tuition income sources such as donations or event proceeds for comprehensive financial oversight.

Promo

Design promotional offers (e.g., first-month discounts) to attract new enrollments and boost marketing efforts.
